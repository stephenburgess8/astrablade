What do these mean?

  • Reserve officer is the tank / highest strength character... not too smart or charming though.
  • Merchant trader is wily, and is supported by a caravan who stick together.
  • Inventor uses technology to protect her/himself and keep enemies at a distance with powerful ranged attacks.
  • Martial Arts Adept is a master of hand-to-hand combat but lacks wealth or charisma.
  • Freelancer doesn't have much of anything except luck and lots of endurance.
